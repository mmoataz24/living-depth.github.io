The Concept

Architecture that
moves like water.

Rising on the Red Sea shore beside Wadi El Gemal National Park, The Living Depth draws its form from the hydrodynamic streamlines of fish in motion and the radial geometry of a starfish. Every surface is shaped to guide visitors downward — from sunlit galleries to the silence of the deep.
